Imagination

GUIDE TO HELP YOU USE YOUR IMAGINATION

"By using your imagination, it can make your day much more exciting and interesting. You'll find yourself more motivated and happy. Most adults grow out of their imagination, therefore they think they don't have one. By learning to take control of your imagination and by expanding it, you will be feeling much more calm and carefree."

"Imagination is more important thank knowledge." - Albert Einstein

  • Think about doing something out of the ordinary when you wake up.
  • Get a really close friend to play a game with you called "Gibberish".
  • Take a ten minute break during the day to go outside, sit on a bench, and watch people walk by.
  • Pretend you have a super cool superpower that you have to hide from people.

Source: How to Use Your Imagination
